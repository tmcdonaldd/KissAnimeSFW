Where to Find Legal Content

There are a variety of places to look for legal and supportive ways to stream your favorite anime content. Some popular streaming services to watch anime on in the U.S. are Crunchyroll, Funimation, Hulu, Netflix, and more. By watching your shows through these services a specific percentage of the profits from these streaming sites goes back towards the original animation studios, the creators, and more importantly the animators themeselves. If you would like a more specific list of resources please see the sidebar for inexpensive streaming sites to check out!

Ways to Support Your Favorite Creators

You can support your favorite anime creators in many ways outside of streaming their content on legal and supportive services. Some of these other methods include, but are not limited to, attending live events, buying their merchandise from official stores, donating or commissioning their work, and more! Under the resources tab is a link to a fully comprehensive list to all the options available to you. Remember that by supporting the animation industry you are supporting a more secure and valuable future for your favorite content!
